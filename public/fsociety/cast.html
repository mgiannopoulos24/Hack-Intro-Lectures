Main Cast

Elliot Alderson

Played by Rami Malek

A cybersecurity engineer and vigilante hacker with social anxiety disorder and clinical depression. Elliot is recruited by Mr. Robot to join fsociety, a group of hacktivists aiming to cancel all consumer debt by destroying E Corp's data.

Darlene Alderson

Played by Carly Chaikin

Elliot's sister and a skilled hacker. Darlene is one of the founding members of fsociety and leads the group in times of crisis. She has a complex relationship with her brother and shares his desire to take down E Corp.

Mr. Robot

Played by Christian Slater

The mysterious leader of fsociety who recruits Elliot to join his anarchist hacker group. He has a personal vendetta against E Corp and seeks to erase all consumer debt by destroying the data held by the company.

Angela Moss

Played by Portia Doubleday

Elliot's childhood friend and an employee at E Corp. Angela's mother died as a result of an E Corp chemical leak, which fuels her complicated relationship with the company. Her journey takes her from an idealistic young professional to someone much more complex.

Tyrell Wellick

Played by Martin Wallström

A highly ambitious and tech-savvy executive at E Corp. Tyrell is obsessed with power and position, but develops an unusual connection with Elliot. His obsession with becoming CTO drives much of his early actions.

Dominique DiPierro

Played by Grace Gummer

An FBI agent investigating the 5/9 hack. Dom is determined and meticulous in her work, often at the expense of her personal life. She becomes increasingly entangled in the conspiracy surrounding fsociety and the Dark Army.

Whiterose / Minister Zhang

Played by BD Wong

The enigmatic leader of the Dark Army and China's Minister of State Security. Whiterose is meticulous about time and has mysterious plans involving scientific research that go beyond the financial revolution of fsociety.

Phillip Price

Played by Michael Cristofer

The CEO of E Corp with a cold and calculated approach to business and power. Price has complex motivations that go beyond corporate success, involving personal vendettas and global economic manipulation.

Joanna Wellick

Played by Stephanie Corneliussen

Tyrell's ambitious and calculating wife who is willing to go to extreme lengths to get what she wants. Cold, calculating and manipulative, she plays a complex role in the series as she searches for her missing husband.

Leslie Romero

Played by Ron Cephas Jones

An older hacker and founding member of fsociety. Romero is a skilled technician with a criminal past and provides much of the technical expertise in fsociety's operations, including setting up their headquarters.

Trenton

Played by Sunita Mani

A young Iranian-American college student and talented hacker who joins fsociety. Trenton is thoughtful and idealistic, hoping that her actions will create a better world. She develops a close relationship with Mobley as they navigate the chaos following the 5/9 hack.

Leon

Played by Joey Bada$$

A mysterious new friend Elliot meets who is obsessed with 90s sitcoms and often philosophizes about their meaning. Leon's true identity and purpose are gradually revealed as the series progresses, showing his connection to larger forces at play.

Mobley

Played by Azhar Khan

A member of fsociety and skilled hacker who works in IT security. Mobley is often anxious and paranoid, especially after the 5/9 hack, and his friendship with Trenton provides an important emotional anchor throughout the chaos they experience.
